The Wireless Information & Intelligence (WILL) lab conducts research on mobile edge computing, edge intelligence, and decentralized/distributed learning in networked systems. We are interested in novel algorithmic designs and fundamental theoretical insights for these areas with rigorous guarantees, as well as practical mobile applications powered by these methods to enhance our everyday lives.
