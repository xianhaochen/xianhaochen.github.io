About Me

Welcome to Zheng Lin's homepage! I received my master's degree at Fudan University, Shanghai, China. I was awarded the Postgraduate National Scholarship (1/165) in 2022 and Shanghai Outstanding Graduate in 2023. I was a member of the Fudan INC lab led by Prof. Yue Gao, and worked closely with Prof. Zhe Chen. I am now a first-year PhD student at The University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Xianhao Chen and co-supervised by Prof. Kaibin Huang.
